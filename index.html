Who are we?

Tutoringisus started in the Bay Area in 2015 by Dr. Mahmoud Kedkad. He put his passion of teaching in helping hundreds of students achieve their academic goals, improve grades and get into the top colleges of their choice. The company grew organically through our dedicated and focused approach to help students at all grade levels. We earned the reputation to be the best, because parents saw a complete change and transformation in their children. As a natural progression working with high schoolers in 2019 tutoringisus added College Counseling, and in 2020 we added International Student program. With the COVID-19 Pandemic, we transformed our tutoring to online and since we have deep roots in technology we immediately brought along the latest of virtual learning to the homes of all of our students and the transition could not go any smoother. Now almost 5 years later tutoringisus has grown to become one of the most successful tutoring companies in the Bay Area, and become only one of few companies that can offer all of the educational services under one roof.
